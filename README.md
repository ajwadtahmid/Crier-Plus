## Overview

Your phone sends reminders you've learned to ignore. Crier Plus makes them impossible to tune out. At the scheduled time, a voice says your name and tells you exactly what to do. No pings. No banners. Just a personalized announcement.

### Key Features

- **Speaks Your Name** — Every reminder is a personal announcement. Your name, your message, spoken aloud at the right moment.

- **AI-Written Messages** — Tap "AI Suggest" and Apple Intelligence generates three message options (friendly, motivating, direct). Pick one, or let it rewrite your message in a different tone. Falls back gracefully when AI is unavailable.

- **Flexible Scheduling** — One-time, daily, weekdays only, weekly, or fully custom days of the week. Each reminder fires exactly when you need it.

- **Voice Preview** — Hear exactly how your reminder will sound before saving. Adjust speech rate and pitch in Settings until it feels right.

- **Completely Offline** — No internet connection required, ever. No server, no account, no subscription. Your reminders live on your device.

## Features

- **Spoken Reminders** — Generates a `.caf` audio file from your message using on-device text-to-speech, attached to the local notification as a custom sound
- **Personalized with Your Name** — Enter your name once on onboarding; every reminder message addresses you directly
- **AI Message Suggestions** — Three tone options (Friendly, Motivating, Direct) generated by Apple Intelligence via FoundationModels; requires iOS 26+ and an Apple Intelligence-capable device
- **Tone Rewriting** — Rewrites your existing message in Friendly, Motivating, Urgent, or Funny tone using on-device AI
- **Template Fallback** — When AI is unavailable, a built-in template (`"[Name], [Task]. [Name], [Task]."`) fills in automatically with no crash or hang
- **Voice Preview** — Live audio playback of any message before committing, using current speech rate and pitch settings
- **Speech Rate & Pitch** — Sliders in Settings let you tune the voice; previews update instantly
- **Repeat Patterns** — None, Daily, Weekdays, Weekly, or Custom (pick any combination of days)
- **Active/Inactive Toggle** — Pause and resume any reminder from the list without deleting it
- **Edit & Reschedule** — Editing a reminder cancels the old notification and audio, then regenerates both cleanly
- **Alarm Ring View** — Full-screen view with animated speaker icon appears when you tap a notification banner
- **Dark Mode** — All colors adapt automatically via named color assets

# 4. Run on a physical device (iOS 17+)

No dependencies to install. No `.env` file. No package manager. Everything ships with iOS.

## Data & Privacy

- **No account required.** The app does not ask for or store your email, Apple ID, or any account credentials.
- **No network requests.** Crier Plus never connects to the internet. There is no backend, no API, and no proxy server.
- **No analytics or crash reporting.** No third-party SDKs are included.
- **Local-only storage.** Your name, reminders, audio files, and preferences are stored on your device only, inside the app sandbox.
- **On-device AI.** Message generation uses Apple Intelligence (FoundationModels), which runs entirely on your device. No text leaves your phone.

Full privacy policy: [Crier-Plus.md](Crier-Plus.md)

## FAQ

**Q: Does this work without internet?**  
Yes. Everything runs on-device — text-to-speech, audio generation, notification scheduling, and AI message writing. No internet connection is ever required.

**Q: What is the AI Suggest feature?**  
It uses Apple Intelligence (FoundationModels, iOS 26+) to write spoken reminder messages in three tones. If your device doesn't support Apple Intelligence, the AI buttons are hidden and a simple template is used instead.

**Q: Which devices support AI Suggest?**  
iPhone 15 Pro / iPhone 16 and later, and Apple silicon iPad models, with iOS/iPadOS 26+ and Apple Intelligence enabled in Settings.

**Q: Can I change how the voice sounds?**  
Yes. Go to Settings → Voice Settings and adjust Speech Rate and Pitch. Tap the speaker icon to preview before saving.

**Q: What happens if I change my name in Settings?**  
The app will ask if you want to regenerate spoken audio for all existing reminders with your new name. Progress is shown live as each reminder updates.

**Q: Will reminders fire if the app is closed?**  
Yes. Reminders are scheduled as local notifications and fire even if the app is force-quit from the app switcher.

**Q: What audio format is used?**  
Reminders are generated as `.caf` files using `AVSpeechSynthesizer` and stored in the app's `Library/Sounds/` directory for use with `UNNotificationSound`.

## Known Limitations

- **Simulator audio** — Custom notification sounds don't play on Simulator; test on a real device.
- **Apple Intelligence** — AI features require iOS 26+ and an Apple Intelligence-capable device. Not available on older hardware.
- **Background audio generation** — Audio files are generated when you save a reminder, not in the background. If you're offline or low on storage, save may fail.
- **Repeat reminders and audio** — All repeat occurrences of a reminder use the same audio file. Changing your name requires manually updating affected reminders via Settings.
